»Overview of Examination Gloves

↪Examination gloves are available either sterile or non-sterile, while surgical gloves are generally sterile.

↪Besides medicine, medical gloves are widely used in chemical and biochemical laboratories. Medical gloves offer some basic protection against corrosives and surface contamination.

↪However, they are easily penetrated by solvents and various hazardous chemicals, and should not be used for dishwashing or otherwise when the task involves immersion of the gloved hand in the solvent.

↪Medical gloves are recommended to be worn for two main reasons:

↪To reduce the risk of contamination of health-care workers hands with blood and other body fluids

↪To reduce the risk of germ dissemination to the environment and of transmission from the health-care worker to the patient and vice versa, as well as from one patient to another.
